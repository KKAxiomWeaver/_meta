## 06 â€” Risks & Limitations

Complexity Corridor OS must manage several risks:

- **Over-concentration Risk**  
  Over-relying on a single RCC Corridor can create **centralized vulnerability** if compromised.

- **Urban & Land-Use Constraints**  
  Existing land rights, economic zones, and social patterns limit where corridors can realistically be defined or adjusted.

- **Civilian Stress & Complexity**  
  Overdesigning complexity along corridors without adequate Habitat OS semantics may confuse residents.

- **Inflexibility Risk**  
  Too rigid adherence to RCC Corridors can hinder future infrastructure needed for economic or social reasons.

CC-OS explicitly avoids:

- Treating corridors as military-exclusive spaces.  
- Advocating major, disruptive urban redesign solely for complexity purposes.  
- Ignoring accessibility, inclusivity, and livability in corridor design.
